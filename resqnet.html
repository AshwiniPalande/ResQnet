Score Formula

  freq_trend
  × intensity_proj
  × pop_exposure
  × (1 − adaptive_cap)
) → normalised 0–100

freq_trend = recent_rate / hist_rate
intensity_proj = CMIP6 mean multiplier
pop_exposure = DiCRA exposure index
adaptive_cap = DiCRA adaptive capacity
